Homework: Class 4

For this class's homework, you have two options. Pick one of the two plugins below and add it to your site. For extra credit, add both!

Option 1: FancyBox, a Lightbox Plugin

Have you ever been to a site where when you open a video, it appears in a pop-up box on top of the rest of the page? This is a plugin that lets you add this feature to your own site! You'll need to find a video on youtube (one featuring the celebrity or superhero from your website!) and then follow the instructions below.

Option 2: funnyText, a...Funny Text Plugin!

funnyText allows you to create some fun text effects on your site. You really should check it out to get an idea of what it does.
